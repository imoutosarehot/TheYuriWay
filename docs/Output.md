### Early Output

i started output at like basically the same time i started learning japanese and i came out fine. everyone who says that early output will ruin your japanese is retarded and coping and a stupid introverted incel with no friends. there are lots of examples of people who started output early on and are still good at japanese. from my personal obeservations early outputters actually tend to be better cause they have wayyyyyy more practice than people who avoided it. there are also late outputters who are good but i dont think it makes a difference at all.

### Starting Output

starting output is usually pretty hard unless you are really confident in yourself and dont mind sounding retarded, but you kinda just need to work through it one way or another.

i personally recommend [vrchat](VRChat.md), there are lots of japanese worlds and even one on one conversation rooms if you are scared of groups (groups are also harder to practice in at the start since there is less room for you to speak). you can also find native speakers online pretty easily, but thats mostly gonna be text which is fine ig but using your voice is good.

outputting with other learners is a complete waste of time imo but its not gonna ruin your japanese or whatever so idk have fun ig

also remember to output for the sake of actually interacting with people, istg people who output just for the sake of getting better have the most こんにちわ　元気ですか？　今日なにしたの？？？ ahh conversations ever and its shallow asf and they never make any friends and give up. just make friends and have authentic conversations with people and youll get way further!!!!
