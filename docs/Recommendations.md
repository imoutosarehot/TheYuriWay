### reading recommendations

the general pipeline for reading will be light novels/visual novels and then normal novels. basically all yuri light novels and visual novels are piss easy slice of life slop, and normal novels aren't significantly harder. there are thousands of options here so I will try to list my favorites as well as popular/significant ones. you can also read manga but its not very good immersion imo. worth it for the yuri though.

for beginners:

- [週に一度クラスメイトを買う話](https://bookmeter.com/books/20580019) ln, perfect beginner novel
- [女同士とかありえないでしょ](https://bookmeter.com/books/14915752) ln
- [安達としまむら](https://bookmeter.com/books/6508788) ln, very popular but kind of boring in my opinion
- [わたしが恋人になれるわけないじゃん、ムリムリ!](https://bookmeter.com/books/15079821) ln
- [屋上の百合霊さん](https://vndb.org/v8508) vn, really cute
- [はなひらっ！](https://vndb.org/v5244) vn, probably the easiest option on this list by far
- [FLOWERS](https://vndb.org/v14267) vn, haven't played this but it's quite popular
- [君と彼女のリリィヴァガンザ](https://vndb.org/v52341) vn
- [真愛の百合は赤く染まる](https://vndb.org/v26232) vn, schizo gore yuri, personal favourite
- [citrus](https://anilist.co/manga/80145/citrus/) manga, classic yuri
- [魔法少女にあこがれて](https://anilist.co/manga/109017/) manga, ecchi peak
- [やがて君になる](https://anilist.co/manga/86218/) manga, classic yuri
- [一度だけでも、後悔してます。](https://anilist.co/manga/110530/) manga, idr what this was about but it was good i think
- [明るい記憶喪失](https://anilist.co/manga/98188/) manga, really cute
- [きたない君がいちばんかわいい](https://anilist.co/manga/110396/) manga, toxic yuri, best yuri ever made probably
- [今日はカノジョがいないから](https://anilist.co/manga/135802/) manga, cheating yuri and extremely peak
- [私の推しは悪役令嬢。](https://anilist.co/manga/109206/) manga, fantasy yuri and also has a novel
- [ぜんぶ壊して地獄で愛して](https://anilist.co/manga/161477/) manga, more toxic yuri
- [陰キャギャルでもイキがりたい！](https://anilist.co/manga/156935/) manga, idr much but it was cute
- [あくまであまい私の彼女](https://anilist.co/manga/176854/) manga, really cute vampire (?) yuri
- [繭、纏う](https://anilist.co/manga/108228/) manga

slightly harder:
